Photos from our community

These photos were taken by photographers from our community. They showcase the beauty and vibrancy of our town square, museum, and housing areas. Each image captures a unique aspect of our community, highlighting the rich culture and history that Naawi Oodena represents.

Why these photos

We chose these photos to represent our community because they reflect our values and aspirations. The town square is a gathering place for our community, the museum preserves our history, and the housing areas symbolize our commitment to creating a safe and welcoming environment for all residents. These images serve as a reminder of our shared journey.
